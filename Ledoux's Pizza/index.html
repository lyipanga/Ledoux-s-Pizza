About Us

At Ledoux's, a love of good food, good people, and good times has always been at the heart of what we do. Launched in 2021, the concept was simple: to serve delicious, honest burgers, sides and drinks using quality ingredients at an affordable price. Our first 50-seat store in Malden was a roaring success and the rest is history.
